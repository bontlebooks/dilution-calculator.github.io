C1V1 = C2V2 Formula Explained

The formula C1V1 = C2V2 is the standard way to calculate dilutions in chemistry. It shows that the amount of solute stays the same before and after dilution.

  • C1: initial concentration of the solution
  • V1: initial volume of the solution
  • C2: desired final concentration
  • V2: desired final volume
