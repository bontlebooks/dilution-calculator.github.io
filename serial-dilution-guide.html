Serial Dilution Guide

A serial dilution is a stepwise dilution of a substance in solution. Each step reduces the concentration, which is useful in labs for experiments requiring precise concentrations.

Steps:

  1. Decide the dilution factor for each step (e.g., 1:10).
  2. Use the C1V1 = C2V2 formula or our calculator for each step.
  3. Transfer the calculated volume to the next tube or container and add solvent.
  4. Repeat until the desired concentration is reached.
